Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need technical experience to take this course?

No prior technical experience is required. Our course is designed for beginners and starts with the fundamentals. Basic computer literacy (ability to use a computer for everyday tasks) is helpful, but we'll teach you everything else you need to know.

How much time should I dedicate per week?

For the full-time program, expect to dedicate 25-30 hours per week. For the part-time program, we recommend 12-15 hours per week including classes and assignments.

Will there be job placement assistance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive career support including resume building, interview preparation, and connections with our hiring partners. Our placement rate is over 85% within three months of course completion.

Is there a certificate upon completion?

Yes, upon successfully completing the course and final project, you'll receive an industry-recognized IT Basic Skills certification from White Fire. We also help prepare students for popular industry certifications like CompTIA A+.
