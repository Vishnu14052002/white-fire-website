Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need programming experience to take this course?

While some basic programming knowledge is helpful, our course starts with Python fundamentals to ensure everyone can follow along. If you're completely new to programming, we recommend taking our introductory Python course first.

How much time should I dedicate per week?

For the full-time program, expect to dedicate 30-40 hours per week. For the part-time program, we recommend 15-20 hours per week including classes and assignments.

Will there be job placement assistance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive career support including resume building, interview preparation, and connections with our hiring partners. Our placement rate is over 85% within three months of course completion.

Is there a certificate upon completion?

Yes, upon successfully completing the course and final project, you'll receive an industry-recognized Data Science certification from White Fire.
